Vega: Gnocchi with spinach, ricotta, Parmesan cheese, pine nuts, lime, parsley en pepper


Ingredients

Amount Ingredients
Hand gnocchi
2 Tbsp ricotta
Hand parmesan cheese
1 Tbsp pine nuts
½ lemon
Some pepper
Some fresh parsley

How to Make It

Cook the gnocchi in 2-4 minutes. Stir-fry the spinach with olive oil until it shrinks down. Roast some pine nuts in an old frypan (no oil, and make sure they don’t burn). Add some pepper to it. Pour the water of the gnocchi and add it to the spinach. Not mandatory: Stir-fry it for a few additional minutes, so the gnocchi gets slightly crispy. Place te gnocchi and spinach on a plate, scoop some ricotta over it, add quite some parmesan cheese flakes and top it off with some pine nuts. DONE!
